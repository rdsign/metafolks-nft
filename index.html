Welcome to Metafolks!

Metafolks is a collection of 10,000 unique and programmatically generated NFTs from over 600 possible traits, unique digital collectibles living on the Polygon blockchain, making each of them very special. They are all unique, but some are rarer than others.

The Metafolks are stores as ERC-721 tokens on the Polygon blockchain and hosted on IPFS. Once released, each Metanoid will cost 8 MATIC.
